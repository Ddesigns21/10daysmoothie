Yes/No Quiz

1. Do you crave sweets, bread, pasta, white rice, and/or potatoes?

2. Do you eat processed foods (TV dinners, lunchmeats, bacon, canned soup, snack bars) or fast foods at least three times a week?

3. Do you drink caffeinated beverages like coffee and tea more than twice daily?

4. Do you drink diet sodas or use artificial sweeteners at least once a day?

5. Do you sleep less than eight hours per day?

6. Do you drink less than 64 ounces of good, clean water daily?

7. Are you very sensitive to smoke, chemicals, or fumes in the environment?

8. Have you ever taken antibiotics, antidepressants, or other medications?

9. Have you ever taken birth control pills or other estrogens, such as hormone replacement therapy?

10. Do you have frequent yeast infections?
